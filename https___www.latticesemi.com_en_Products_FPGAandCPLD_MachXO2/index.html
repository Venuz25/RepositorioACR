The Low Power Programmable Leader

OUR PRODUCTS

Lattice low power FPGAs and easy-to-use software tools enable designers to innovate and reduce development time for their Industrial, Automotive, Communications, Computing, and Consumer applications.
Lattice Certus-N2 family of small, general purpose FPGAs optimized for low power relative to the competition and supports industry-leading design security in its class.
Lattice Radiant software utilizes a unified design database, design constraints flow, and timing analysis to ensure consistent optimization and analysis with optimal results.
CertusPro-NX Sensor to Ethernet Bridge Board is fully integrated with NVIDIA IGX/AGX™ system software offering open-source enablement IP and programmable system control.

OUR SOLUTION STACKS

Whether you're designing for a smart factory, advanced driver experiences, AI datacenters, leading-edge telecom infrastructure, or connected home, Lattice Solution Stacks allow you to quickly unleash your innovation.
The Lattice Sentry solution stack enables a cyber resilient and NIST SP 800-193 compliant PFR solution with easy-to-use FPGA design tools and a full suite of custom design services.
The Lattice Drive solution stack is optimized for driving development of embedded display processing solutions, and enables rapid adoption of FPGAs for automotive applications.
The Lattice sensAI solution stack includes everything you need to evaluate, develop, and deploy FPGA-based Machine Learning / Artificial Intelligence solutions.
